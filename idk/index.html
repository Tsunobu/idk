What is an Ecosystem?

An ecosystem is a biological community of the organisms interacting with their environment

Experiment Objective

To find out about the significance of a complete ecosystem and the dynamics in it.

How Earthworms Affect Plant Growth

By their activity in the soil, earthworms offer many benefits: increased nutrient availability, better drainage, and a more stable soil structure, all of which help improve farm productivity. Worms feed on plant debris (dead roots, leaves, grasses, manure) and soil.

Hypothesis

The plants with worms will grow better and faster because the presence of earthworms in the soil enhances plant growth and vitality through improved soil structure, nutrient cycling, microbial activity, water retention, and reduced soil compaction.
